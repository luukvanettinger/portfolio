Socialwall

  • Node.js
  • Photoshop
  • UX/UI

Fontys wanted to put more attention into their social media channels and ensure that more students were aware of the existence of these channels. I designed a social wall for the new building that students walk past every day and stay up to date with current news from Fontys. The end goal here was to get more followers. Fontys can adjust the content themselves with a cms that is connected to a node.js-driven database.
